# z13-black-dahlia

Assortment of visualizations for Black Dahlia episode of Let's Crack Zodiac.  Click the links for live demos.

- [Columnar transposition visualizer](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/columnar-transposition-visualizer.html): An animation demonstrating a simple columnar transposition example.
- [Dots neighborhood visualizer](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/dots-neighborhood-visualizer.html): Attempt to compare the scale of 100 "things" vs 500,000 "things."
- [Name animator](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/name-animator.html): Displays a cluster of random 13-character names whose letter frequencies match the cipher symbol frequencies of Z13.
- [Nine Z13 possibilities](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/nine-possibilities.html): A speculation of nine possible categories of answers to Z13.
- [Permutation grid](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/permutation-grid.html): Shows the scale of one permutation found amongst 5,040 permutations.
- [Permutation keyword samples](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/permutation-keyword-samples.html): A sampling of keywords that work just as well as "LIABETH" for Baber's Z13 permutation.
- [Sockpuppet timeline](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/sock-timeline.html): Experimental visualization of timelines of comments posted by suspected sockpuppet accounts on YouTube.
- [Z13 substitution key visualizer](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/substitution-key-visualizer.html): Visualization of how ciphertext and plaintext alphabets could be configured for Z13 via various keywords.
- [Texas sharpshooter fallacy (variant 1)](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/texas-sharpshooter-1.html): An animation demonstrating the Texas sharpshooter fallacy. 
- [Texas sharpshooter fallacy (variant 2)](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/texas-sharpshooter-2.html): Same animation but without the skewed angle.
- [Thanos "snap" effect](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/thanos-snap.html): Performs a particle fade animation on various images.
- [Trust animation](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/trust-animation.html): Shows the idea of "broken trust" by animating the letters of the word "trust" falling away.
- [Word cluster viewer](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/word-cluster-viewer.html): Simple viewer for a very large image of a cluster of names.
- [Z13 Baber substitution key interpretations](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/z13-baber-substitution-key-interpretation.html): Visualizes different interpretations of cipher symbols that are possible using the scheme demonstrated by Ed Girgio et. al.
- [Z13 decryptor](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/z13-decryptor.html): Visualizes decrypting various simple substitution solutions for Z13.
- [Z13 grid decipherment visualizer](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/z13-grid-decipherment-visualizer.html): Visualizer for Baber's Z13 grid cipher solution, plus an assortment of variants and alternate solutions.
- [Z13 shuffler](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/z13-shuffle.html): Animates the scrambling of the Z13 cipher plus variants.
- [Z18 Hefner solution visualizer](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/z18-visualizer.html): Animation demonstrating aspects of Thomas Hefner's Z18 solution proposal.
- [Z32 solutions](https://doranchak.github.io/z13-black-dahlia/z32-solutions.html): Displays bubbles of randomly selected Z32 solutions.
